# Content Strategy

## Voice

Plain-language, civic, respectful, local, and nonpartisan.

## Reader Needs

- Understand what RCV is.
- Understand how an RCV count works.
- Understand why some residents might support it.
- Understand concerns and tradeoffs.
- Understand that the home rule petition pathway requires review.

## Placeholder Rule

Content may describe intended future sections, but it must not present unverified claims as settled facts.
