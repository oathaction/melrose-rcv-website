# Research Plan

## Research Goals

- Identify primary sources for Massachusetts home rule petitions.
- Identify Melrose municipal election context from official sources.
- Collect reliable explanations of ranked-choice voting.
- Document open questions for legal and election-administration review.

## Source Priorities

- Massachusetts state sources.
- City of Melrose sources.
- Official election administration sources.
- Nonpartisan civic education sources.

## Open Questions

- What local votes, filings, and state approvals would be required?
- What election systems and administrative changes would need review?
- What timeline assumptions are reasonable only after source review?
