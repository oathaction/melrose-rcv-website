# Contact Melrose RCV

Have a question about ranked-choice voting, the home rule petition path, or something on this site? Send it in. Skeptical questions are welcome.

## Good reasons to reach out

- You want to understand how RCV works.
- You have a concern the FAQ does not answer.
- You found a source issue or wording that needs correction.
- You want to volunteer.
- You want to host or help with a neighborhood conversation.
- You want updates about public meetings or campaign next steps.

## What to include

Tell us whether you are asking as a resident, volunteer, local stakeholder, or source reviewer. If you are flagging a factual issue, include the page, the sentence, and the source you think we should review.
