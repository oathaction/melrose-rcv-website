# About Melrose RCV

Melrose RCV is a nonpartisan civic education and organizing effort working to build public understanding and support for bringing ranked-choice voting to Melrose municipal elections through a Massachusetts home rule petition.

The purpose of this site is to help neighbors understand what RCV is, how it works, why supporters think it is worth considering, and what legal and implementation questions need review before any Melrose proposal is final.

## What this site is

- A resident-facing education resource.
- A source-grounded organizing hub.
- A place to ask questions, volunteer, and follow the home rule petition path.

## What this site is not

- It is not an official City of Melrose election resource.
- It is not legal advice.
- It is not final ballot instruction.
- It does not claim Melrose has adopted RCV.
- It does not claim implementation would be automatic.

## How we handle claims

The site should use official sources for legal, municipal, and election-administration claims. Research and civic-design sources can support implementation and education guidance. Advocacy sources can help explain supporter arguments, but they should not stand alone for legal, cost, equipment, or local administrative claims.

## How residents can participate

Residents can join updates, ask a question, volunteer, help review plain-language materials, share source-backed information, and attend public conversations.
