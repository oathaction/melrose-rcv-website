# Melrose RCV Project Brief

Melrose RCV is a nonpartisan civic education and community organizing effort exploring ranked-choice voting for Melrose municipal elections through a Massachusetts home rule petition.

## Audience

- Melrose residents learning what RCV is.
- Volunteers and civic organizers.
- Local officials and election administrators reviewing questions.
- Skeptical or undecided residents looking for clear answers.

## Current Phase

Initial scaffold only. The website should compile and show placeholder routes, but the content is not ready for public launch.

## Required Guardrails

- Melrose has not been described as having adopted RCV.
- Any home rule petition path is presented as a process requiring review.
- Sources are placeholders until verified.
- Legal and election-administration details require appropriate review.
